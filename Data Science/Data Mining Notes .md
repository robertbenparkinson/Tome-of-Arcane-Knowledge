## Calculating Distances

### Manhattan Distance

|x1 - x2| + |y1 - y2| 

for example: (4,4) to (1,1)

|4-1| + |4-1| = 6

### Euclidean Distance

((x1-x2)^2 + (y1-y2)^2)^1/2 

for example: (4,4) to (1,1)

((4-1)^2 + (4-1)^2)^1/2
((3)^2 + (3)^2)^1/2
(9 + 9)^1/2
(18)^1/2 = 4.24

### Minkowski Distance Metric

d(x,y) = (nEk=1 |xk - yk|r)^1/r

$ \sqrt{\left(x_{1} - x_{2}\right) + \left(y_{1} - y_{2}\right)} $
